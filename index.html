Survey & Design

At Oxynet Ltd we believe in proper route survey, which will capture important critical features to be included in the proposed fiber optic cable route drawing, showing information about terrain details, obstacles, and quantities. This entire process leads to a valuable cost predictor and implementation guide. From proposed fiber optic route surveys to drafting and drawing of the surveys, Oxynet Company Ltd also provides the expertise needed in construction plans for trenching,coupling, backfilling, fiber optic cable pulling, and fiber optic cable termination. Our tools involve geographic information systems mapping software, aerial imaging, AutoCAD MAP, Mapinfo, and Google Earth, various owner utilized proprietary software.

Fibre Splicing, Termination & Testing
