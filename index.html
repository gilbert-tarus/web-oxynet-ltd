About Us

The company was incorporated under the laws of Kenya in October 2021 to carry out business of Optical Fiber installation, Structured Cabling, survey and design amongst several IT solutions.

Oxynet aims at providing a wide range of advanced telecommunications solutions to leading operators and vendors in Kenya.

Why Choose Us

  • MISSION
    To become a leading provider of Telecommunication solutions in both private and public sector in the region. This will involve supporting and working with establishments to conceptualize formulate, execute, implement and operate quality, productive, efficient and cost-effective information and communication system solutions.

  • VISION
    To collaborate and partner with players in Telecommunication industry to address and support the implementation of information and communication system needs, goals and cost objectives of establishments effectively and at world class standards.
